Join us

We welcome motivated scientists interested in rigorous, creative research.

Interested candidates are encouraged to contact Di to discuss potential research opportunities or collaborations.

Graduate students

Prospective students excited by nucleic acid engineering, structural biology, chemical biology, and therapeutic design are encouraged to reach out and apply through ASU graduate programs.

Postdoctoral researchers

The lab is seeking a postdoctoral researcher to lead RNA structure projects using cryo-EM. Prior cryo-EM experience is helpful but not required; scientific rigor and eagerness to learn are essential.

Undergraduate researchers

Undergraduate positions are limited because each student receives close mentoring. Barrett Honors students interested in thesis work are especially encouraged to plan ahead.

How to reach out

Send a concise note about your interests.

Please include your background, the research direction that interests you, and relevant experience or goals. Attach a CV when appropriate.
